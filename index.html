About Me


Hi! I am in my last undergraduate year studying computer science at UBC.

I'm always eager to explore new areas in computer science. My research interests are human-computer interaction, machine learning, cybersecurity, blockchain technology, and software practices. I want to create novel interfaces that would provide accessibility for people of diverse backgrounds.

You can usually find me lurking in the lab, playing badminton, or reviewing Japanese in Math Club... I also frequently attend Hackathons and enjoy challenging myself with various competitive programming and security contests. I am an active participant of Designing for People, UBC ICPC Competitive Programming Club, UBC Launchpad, UBC Emerging Media Lab, Undergraduate Mathematics Society, Computer Science Student Society, and UBC Capture the Flag

If any of our interests align, feel free to chat with me on Linkedin!
